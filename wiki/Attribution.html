# Abstract

We are a positive and loving team. From the beginning of the project design, all team members actively participate in project construction and take advantage of their abilities. Through discussion and cooperation, we have made a lot of progress both within and outside the competition. We developed a close and trusting relationship and became very good friends.

# Human Practice Support

Zhaoyu Zhou, a teacher in the School of Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Tongji University. She used to be a designer who focused on women. We appreciate her sincere suggestions on the commercialization of our project.

Yishu Wu, a senior majoring in Economics, participated in an on-campus innovation and entrepreneurship competition, whose project was about an App designed for women's period and health. We discussed her business plan and the talk gave both of us inspiration.

Professor Jing and Professor Sun, are famous for their project on the mental health of the elderly in rural areas. They offer us an insightful overview of rural issues based on their long-term fieldwork.

Yang, a junior majoring in biology, is one of the organizers of the NoPeriodShaming Program in Fudan. We talk to learn about the NPSP 's story and exchange our opinion about the difficulties women have met nowadays.

Emma is the founder of Relief, which is a cutting-edge lingerie brand dedicated to providing women with comfortable, healthy, and sustainable underwear products driven by innovative technologies. We interviewed her through WeChat and got advice on commercialization and marketing.

Two debate teams from Shanghai Medical College of Fudan University and Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine. They participate in the debate of "SHOULD FREE MENSTRUAL PRODUCTS TO BE LEGISLATED IN CHINA", their opinions did also inspire us to a deeper thinking degree.

Shen Ke is a professor on population policy and intergenerational relationships. We interviewed her after her online lecture, getting a gender view on the seemingly glorious Opening and Reform policy, and learned that inequality might be created even it was long thought to be a good-willed policy.
